## Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard:

- **Be respectful**: no harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory behavior.
- **Be constructive**: critique ideas and code, not people.
- **Assume good intent**: clarify before escalating.
- **Honor privacy**: do not share private logs, data, or credentials.

Maintainers may moderate issues/PRs (edit, close, or block) to keep discussion productive and safe.
